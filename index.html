About us

Welcome to English Future where you can embark on a transformative learning experience designed to take you from beginner to advanced levels in both English and Computer skills. Our comprehensive curriculum covers English language proficiency, TOEFL and IELTS exam preparation, and a journey from basic computer literacy to coding mastery.

Courses

Diploma in English Language

The Diploma in English Teaching is designed to equip individuals with the necessary knowledge and skills to become effective English language educators. The program focuses on both theoretical and practical aspects of teaching English as a second language, preparing graduates to teach learners of various proficiency levels.

Certificate in Information Technology

The "CIT" course aims to provide participants with a fundamental understanding of computer basics, essential software applications, and basic digital literacy skills.

DIT

This diploma program aims to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of information technology concepts while also equipping them with practical skills in graphic design, particularly using software like Adobe Photoshop. Graduates will be prepared for roles that require a blend of IT knowledge and graphic design proficiency

Web Development

The Web Development Bootcamp is designed to provide participants with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in web development. The primary goal is to equip students with the abilities to design, build, and deploy dynamic and interactive websites and web applications.

TOEFL 45 days preparation

The TOEFL Preparation Class aims to help individuals enhance their English language skills and prepare for the TOEFL test. The primary goal is to familiarize students with the test format, improve their proficiency in the English language, and provide strategies for success in each section of the TOEFL exam.

IELTS 45 days preparation

The IELTS Preparation Class is designed to help individuals improve their English language skills and successfully prepare for the IELTS test. The primary goal is to familiarize students with the test format, enhance their proficiency in the English language, and provide strategies for achieving high scores in each section of the IELTS exam.

Duolingo 30 days preparation

The Duolingo English Test Preparation Class aims to help individuals enhance their English language skills and prepare for the Duolingo English Test. The primary goal is to familiarize students with the test format, improve their proficiency in the English language, and provide strategies for success in each section of the DET.
